    Mage: the Ascension has been around as a role playing game for more than 20 years. It is set in a mirror world from ours called the World of Darkness, where horrors could be lurking around every corner. Mages are the hope in this desperate world. They are the activists who fight back in a corrupt system. They are the healers, and the visionaries. Mages are the people who die for what they believe in. Mage the Ascension is a game where you become a character with the powers of a God. Mages reshape reality using their will, and believe so strongly they are able to change the world. Still, mages are just people, and with power comes corruption.
